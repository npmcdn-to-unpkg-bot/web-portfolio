What rabbits eat

Food
My favorite brand for food, hay, treats, and toys is Oxbow.
Fresh veggies
I typically give my rabbits organice kale, parsley, and dandelion greens.
Hay
The best hay for rabbits is Timothy hay; I also give them some Orchard Grass hay to add some variety to their diet.

Fun things about rabbits

  • They do a binky when they are happy (here is a video of Buffy as a baby doing a binky)
  • They love banana, but only in small amounts so it won't make them sick.
  • Their favorite place to be pet in on their head and neck around their ears.
  • They hate to be held and picked up.
  • They love to play tag or hide and seek.
